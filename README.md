# Hotel_Booking_Data_Analysis
Analyze booking trends, cancellations, and revenue patterns to improve business performance.
#Key Observed Trends
<li>Travel agent channels generate the highest number of bookings but also show the highest cancellations.</li> 
<li>Standard rooms contribute the largest share of total revenue due to higher booking volume.</li>
<li>Booking demand varies across months, indicating seasonal travel behavior.</li>
